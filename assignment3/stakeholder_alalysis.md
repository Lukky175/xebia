# Stakeholder Analysis

Stakeholder Identification

| Stakeholder            | Description                                                                          |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Students               | Primary users seeking jobs, internships, and placement opportunities.                |
| Placement Officers     | Manage placement activities, job postings, and student eligibility.                  |
| Recruiters/Companies   | Organizations hiring students through the platform.                                  |
| Faculty Mentors        | Faculty members who guide students in placement preparation.                         |
| College Administration | Decision-makers interested in placement performance and analytics.                   |
| Alumni (Optional)      | Former students who may provide mentorship, referrals, and networking opportunities. |

---

# Stakeholder Analysis Table

| Stakeholder            | Responsibilities                                | Goals                                                        | Challenges                                                       |
| ---------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Students               | Create profiles, upload resumes, apply for jobs | Secure employment opportunities and improve career prospects | Finding suitable jobs, resume preparation, tracking applications |
| Placement Officers     | Manage placement drives and student records     | Improve placement rates and automate processes               | Manual screening and administrative workload                     |
| Recruiters             | Post jobs and evaluate candidates               | Hire qualified candidates efficiently                        | Filtering large numbers of applicants                            |
| Faculty Mentors        | Monitor and guide students                      | Improve student readiness and employability                  | Limited visibility into student progress                         |
| College Administration | Review placement statistics and performance     | Enhance institutional reputation and placement outcomes      | Lack of centralized reporting and analytics                      |
| Alumni                 | Mentor students and provide referrals           | Support student career growth                                | Maintaining engagement with students                             |

---

# Stakeholder Needs

## Students

* User-friendly dashboard
* AI-based job recommendations
* Resume analysis and scoring
* Application tracking
* Interview preparation assistance

## Placement Officers

* Student database management
* Automated eligibility filtering
* Placement reports and analytics
* Communication tools

## Recruiters

* Job posting system
* Candidate search and filtering
* AI-assisted candidate ranking
* Interview scheduling

## Faculty Mentors

* Student performance tracking
* Progress monitoring dashboards
* Placement readiness reports

## College Administration

* Placement statistics
* Performance dashboards
* AI Trend analysis and forecasting

# Stakeholder Influence and Interest Matrix

## High Influence, High Interest

* Placement Officers
* College Administration

These stakeholders directly influence project success and require continuous engagement.

## High Influence, Medium Interest

* Recruiters

Recruiters influence hiring outcomes but interact primarily during recruitment cycles.

## Medium Influence, High Interest

* Students
* Faculty Mentors

These stakeholders are primary users and highly impacted by system functionality.

## Low Influence, Medium Interest
