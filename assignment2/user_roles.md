## Overview

There are three primary user roles on the pf:

1. Job Seeker -> will seek jo b applications
2. Job Provider -> post jobs
3. Admin -> owner and managers of the platform

---

# 1. Job Seeker

## Description

A Job Seeker is candidate searching for internship through the platform.

## Responsibilities

- Create and maintain their profile.
- Search for available internships.
- Apply for internships.
- Track application status.

## Permissions

### Profile Management

- Register and log in.
- Update personal information.
- Upload resume and portfolio.
- Add education and skills.

### Internship Applications

- Browse internship listings.
- Filter internships by location, domain, stipend, and duration.
- Apply for internships.
- Withdraw applications before review.

### Internship Participation

- Submit reports or deliverables.
- Track internship progress.
- View performance evaluations.

---

# 2. Job Provider

## Description

A Job Provider represents a company offering internship opportunities.

## Responsibilities

- Publish internship opportunities.
- Review candidate applications.
- Conduct selection processes.
- Monitor intern performance.
- Provide evaluations and feedback.

## Permissions

### Company Management

- Register company account.
- Manage company profile.
- Add company information and branding.

### Internship Management

- Create internship postings.
- Edit or close internship listings.
- Define eligibility criteria.
- Specify internship duration and stipend.

### Candidate Management

- View applicant profiles.
- Shortlist candidates.
- Reject applications.
- Schedule interviews.
- Send internship offers.

### Internship Monitoring

- Track progress.
- Provide feedback.
- Submit final evaluations.

---

# 3. Admin

## Description

The Admin manages the overall platform.

## Responsibilities

- Manage users and company.
- Moderate internship listings.
- Resolve disputes and complaints.
- Monitor platform health and analytics.

## Permissions

### User Management

- View all users.
- Activate or deactivate accounts.
- Handle reported users.

### Internship Moderation

- Review internship postings.
- Remove fraudulent or inappropriate listings.
- Verify company accounts.

### Platform Management

- Access system analytics.
- Monitor application statistics.
- Manage platform settings.
- Handle support requests.
